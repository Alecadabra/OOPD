Purpose: To calculate the area of a chosen shape, split it to it's square metre, square centimetre and square millimetre components.

Files in project: ShapeCalculator.class, ShapeCalculator.java

Test Files: shapeCalculatorPseudoCode.txt, ShapeCalculator_testResults.txt

Functionality: The program asks for an input to calculate the area of either a triangle, rectangle or circle or to exit the program. The program calculates the area and splits it to it's square metre, square centimetre and square millimetre components and outputs this to the user.

No known bugs
